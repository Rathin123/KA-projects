        <h2 id="compote">Blueberry Compote</h2>
        
        <ul>
            <li>Time: 30-45 min</li>
            <li>Serves: 4</li>
        </ul>
        
        <table class="table">
            <thead>
                <tr>
                    <th>Ingredients</th>
                    <th>Quantity</th>
                </tr>
            </thead>
            <tbody>
                <tr>
                    <td>Frozen Blueberries</td>
                    <td>1 bag</td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>Sugar</td>
                    <td>2.5 cups</td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>Corn Starch</td>
                    <td>6 tablespoons
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>Water</td>
                    <td>6 tablespoons</td>
                </tr>
            </tbody>
        </table>
        
        <p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Add all ingredients to the instant pot.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 2:</strong> Mix well.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 3:</strong> Set the instant pot on high pressure for 10 minutes. Cook.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 4:</strong> Natural release for 10 minutes. Release any remaning pressue with a quick release.
        <br>
        <br>
        Serve with oatmeal, dessert, or any other dishes that come to mind!
</p>

        <h2 id="quesadilla">(Almost) Vegan Quesadillas</h2>
        
        <ul>
            <li>Time: 45-1hr min</li>
            <li>Serves: 4</li>
        </ul>
        
        <table class="table">
            <thead>
                <tr>
                    <th>Ingredients</th>
                    <th>Quantity</th>
                </tr>
            </thead>
            <tbody>
                <tr>
                    <td>10" tortillas</td>
                    <td>4</td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>Soy Cheese</td>
                    <td>3 cups</td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>Roma Tomatoes, finely diced</td>
                    <td>2</td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>Serrano Peppers, finely diced</td>
                    <td>1</td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>Cilantro</td>
                    <td>1/4 cup</td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td>Taco Seasoning</td>
                    <td>2 tbsp</td>
                </tr>
                
            </tbody>
        </table>
        
        <p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Add all diced vegetables to one bowl.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 2:</strong> Heat skillet. Place tortilla on skillet once warm.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 3:</strong> Liberally apply cheese and vegetable mixture to tortilla. Sprinkle taco seasoning on tortilla.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 4:</strong> Fold over tortilla.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 5:</strong> Let cook until both sides are browned. Flip as needed, and check the cheeese for melty consistency.
        <br>
        <br>
        Repeat for other tortillas. Serve with chips and salsa.
</p>
        
    <p><em>Source: <a href="https://www.pressurecookingtoday.com/pressure-cooker-blueberry-compote/">Me!</a></em></p>
